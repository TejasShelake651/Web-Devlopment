Animal World

Deer

Deer are graceful and gentle wild animals. They are known for their long legs, beautiful antlers, and fast running ability. Deer usually live in forests and grasslands.

Dog

Dogs are loyal and friendly animals. They are often called man's best friend. Dogs are intelligent, protective, and loving pets.

Cat

Cats are small and playful animals. They are independent, curious, and love to sleep. Cats are popular pets around the world.

Tiger

Tigers are powerful wild animals. They are known for their orange fur with black stripes. Tigers are strong hunters and live mainly in forests.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals. They have big ears, long trunks, and are very intelligent. Elephants live in herds and care deeply for their families.
